### Complete Patient Journey

**Step 1: Add Patient**
- Click "Patients" tab

- Fill: Name, Email, Phone, DOB, Gender, Address
- Click "Create Patient"
- ✅ Patient appears in patient list

**Step 2: Use AI Diagnosis**
- Click "AI Models" tab
- Click "Open" on Eye Disease Detection
- Upload eye image or paste URL
- Click "Analyze Image"
- Get results: "Diabetic Retinopathy - 94% confidence"
- ✅ Save to patient record

**Step 3: Record Disease**
- Click patient to expand
- Click "Add Disease"
- Enter: Disease name, Date, Notes
- ✅ Disease added to medical history

**Step 4: Schedule Appointment**
- Go to "Appointments" tab
- See appointment listed
- ✅ Can reference in patient record

- Click "Feedback" tab
- Enter subject, type, priority, message
- Click "Send Feedback"
- ✅ Super Admin receives notification

**Step 6: Request Consultation**
- Click "Consultation" tab
- Enter topic, date, time, duration, description
- Click "Book Consultation"
- ✅ Super Admin receives request
- Wait for confirmation
- ✅ Consultation confirmed with date/time
